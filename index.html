// About

Where Radiology
Meets AI

I am a Clinical Professor in the Department of Radiology at Inha University Hospital, specializing in musculoskeletal CT and MR imaging. I also serve as Deputy Chief Medical Officer (DCMO) at DeepNoid, a medical AI company bridging clinical practice with cutting-edge AI technology.

I chair the GIGA Study, a medical AI research group dedicated to validating and advancing AI applications in diagnostic imaging. My work sits at the intersection of clinical radiology, large language models, and healthcare AI policy — exploring how generative AI can augment radiological workflows while maintaining diagnostic integrity.

Currently completing my Ph.D. in Radiology at Inha University Graduate School (Expected Aug 2026)

// Research Interests

Core Themes

🦴

Musculoskeletal Imaging

Shoulder MRI diagnostics, spinal stenosis grading systems, metallic artifact reduction techniques, and radiomics-based biomarker discovery.

🤖

LLM-Based Radiology

Evaluating AI-driven chest X-ray report generation (M4CXR, KARA-CXR) and assessing ChatGPT's capabilities in radiological interpretation.

🧠

Deep Learning Diagnostics

Automated diagnosis of knee osteoarthritis, sacroiliitis detection, and CT-based radiomics for histopathological correlation.

🛡️

AI Safety & Policy

Prompt injection vulnerabilities in medical LLMs, invisible text injection in peer review, and the intersection of AI policy and healthcare governance.
